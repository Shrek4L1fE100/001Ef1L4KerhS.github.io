How to cook good tasting scrambled eggs.


This is how you cook good scrambled eggs with barely any ingredients.🥚

    *Get at least 6 eggs.
    *Buy or use a pan that is high quality or else it will explode
    *Crack and put eggs in a bowl, and mix it
    *Add salt, spices and milk, whatever tastes good.

*Basics done🍳

    *Add oil in your pan and make the stove very hot.🔥
    *Add mixture in pan
    *Wait for 5 seconds
    *Get a wooden or metal spatula thing i dont know what it is called🏑
    *Mix it with the spatula for like 3 minutes idk
    *Eat it silky smooth, or eat it dry i dont care

*Serve at plate or something

*You are done.👏
